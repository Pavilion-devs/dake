Confidential Prediction Markets

The first prediction market where your bets are encrypted on-chain. No one can see which side you picked — not even the blockchain.

The Problem

Prediction markets have a privacy problem

On every existing platform, your bets are public. This creates real consequences.

👁

Front-Running

Whales see your bet, copy your position, and move the odds against you before your trade settles.

💰

Market Manipulation

Traders coordinate based on visible order flow. Large bets shift sentiment before markets resolve.

🧠

Social Pressure

On-chain history reveals your political, financial, and social views to anyone who looks at your wallet.

The Solution

Bet privately. Verify publicly.

Dake uses Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) via Inco Network to encrypt your bet side on-chain. The blockchain verifies everything — without ever seeing your choice.

🔒 Inco FHE

Your YES/NO choice is encrypted using @inco/solana-sdk before hitting the chain. No one — not even validators — can read it.

✅ On-Chain Verification

Winner determination uses encrypted equality (e_eq). The result stays encrypted until the owner decrypts it.
